Introduction to Netflix

In January 2007, Netflix launched a streaming media service, introducing video on demand via the Internet.

Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies on an internet-connected device.

With over 8700 titles and almost 70% of it being movies, users can easily subscribe and gain access to vast content at the ease of their time without the need to adhere to TV schedules.

Netflix's Rise to Popularity

With one of the world's leading entertainment services comprising over 238 million paid memberships in over 190 countries enjoying TV series, films and games across a wide variety of genres and languages. Netflix has been consistently popular in the USA and Canada with over 75 million subscribers in the region alone during its' peak.

Over the years, Netflix has become increasingly popular with its' number of subscribers gradually increasing. From 2019 to 2023, the number of subscribers in the Asia-Pacific have significantly increased from 12 to 39 million subcribers which is 3 times as much. A factor of this is due to India being the second country who produces the most content and is uploaded to Netflix.

Amount of Shows Produced from Each Country

Most shows on Netflix are from the United States of America (USA) as it takes the number one spot for the most produced movies and TV shows with a whopping 3,240 movies and TV shows originating from the USA. Roughly 38% of Netflix's library originates from the USA. Due to the large amount of movies and TV shows on Netflix was from the USA, it's no surprise why the region USA and Canada have the most number of subscribers by area per person.

Second to the USA, India takes the spot for the most movies and TV shows on Netflix with 1,057 shows in Netflix's library making it 12% of Netflix's total show library. There is a great disparity between the second-most and third-most country which is United Kingdom producing movies and TV shows, with a difference of almost half the amount of India. The disparity is even greater between the UK and USA with USA producing 5 times more movies and TV shows that are on Netflix.

Evolution on Show Count and their Demographic

There is a steady increase in the number of movies and TV shows added to Netflix until 2015 where in just a span of 2 years the number of movies and TV shows increased exponentially. Netflix peaked in 2019 in adding movies where in one year they added more than 1400 movies.

Netflix is widely popular among young adults as a survey conducted in the USA proved that 75% of subscribers are between 18 to 34 years old. This is enforced by the fact that most shows are rated TV-MA which stands for TV for Mature Audiences.
