Introduction to Netflix

In January 2007, the company launched a streaming media service, introducing video on demand via the Internet.

Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies on an internet-connected device.

With one of the world's leading entertainment services comprising over 238 million paid memberships in over 190 countries enjoying TV series, films and games across a wide variety of genres and languages.
